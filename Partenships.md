# Partnerships – Ethereum Community Hub Mauritius

The success of the hub relies on strong collaboration with educational institutions, technology communities, and industry partners.

---

# Universities

The hub is currently engaging with universities in Mauritius to explore potential collaborations.

Potential areas of collaboration include:

• Blockchain workshops for students
• Developer training programs
• Joint hackathons
• Research initiatives related to blockchain and cybersecurity

These partnerships will help students transition from academic learning to practical blockchain development.

---

# Local Tech Communities

The hub aims to collaborate with local startup and technology communities to expand awareness and participation in Web3.

These collaborations may include:

• Joint events and meetups
• Technical workshops
• Startup innovation programs

---

# Creative Industry Partnerships

The hub will also collaborate with Mauritian artists and creators through its NFT program.

Goals:

• Help artists tokenize their work
• Introduce blockchain to the creative economy
• Promote Mauritian art to an international audience

---

# Regional Web3 Ecosystem

Through the network of Zedeal Group, the hub is connected with Web3 communities in East Africa.

These connections will allow:

• Cross-community collaboration
• Regional hackathons
• Knowledge sharing between ecosystems

This positions Mauritius as a bridge between African Web3 communities and the global Ethereum ecosystem.
